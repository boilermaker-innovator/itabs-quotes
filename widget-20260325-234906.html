Driveways

A quality concrete driveway handles Rockingham's summer heat and the wear from daily traffic without cracking or crumbling. Dunno Concreting forms, pours and finishes to a professional standard — whether it's a straight run to a single garage or a wide double-entry for a big block. Coloured and exposed finishes available too.

03 / SERVICE

Shed Slabs

Getting a shed slab right in Rockingham means accounting for the sandy coastal soil and correct edge-beam depth so the structure stays level for life. Dunno Concreting handles the prep, formwork, steel reinforcing and pour — leaving you a flat, strong slab ready for your Colorbond shed or workshop.

Patios & Alfresco

Rockingham's outdoor lifestyle calls for a patio you can actually use year-round. Dunno Concreting pours smooth broom-finish or stencilled alfresco slabs that look great under a pergola and hold up to the coastal UV and salt air. Perfect for a BBQ area, outdoor dining or a kids' play zone out back.

Exposed Aggregate

Exposed aggregate is one of the most popular finishes in the southern suburbs — it looks premium, hides dirt well and provides excellent slip resistance around pools and wet areas. Dunno Concreting uses quality local stone mixes and applies a professional sealer to protect your investment from Rockingham's coastal conditions.
