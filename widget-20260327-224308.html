02 / Service

Blocked Drains

Joondalup's sandy soil and coastal tree roots are notorious for infiltrating drain pipes and causing stubborn blockages. Smith Plumbing uses high-pressure jet blasting and CCTV drain cameras to locate and clear the blockage fast — no guesswork, no unnecessary dig-ups. Whether it's the kitchen sink, bathroom, or a main sewer line, we get it flowing again the same day.

03 / Service

Hot Water Systems

Perth's hard water is tough on storage hot water systems — most tanks in Joondalup last 8–12 years before sediment build-up takes its toll. Smith Plumbing supplies and installs gas, electric, and heat pump systems from brands like Rheem, Rinnai, and Dux. We'll advise on the most energy-efficient option for your household size and have your hot water restored before the day is out.

04 / Service

Gas Leaks

A gas leak is a serious emergency — if you smell gas in your Joondalup home, leave immediately and call us. Smith Plumbing's licensed gas fitters respond fast to detect, isolate, and repair gas leaks to Atco and reticulated LPG systems alike. We carry electronic gas detectors and are fully accredited to handle all gas fitting work in Western Australia, keeping your family safe and compliant.

05 / Service

Leaking Taps

A dripping tap wastes up to 20,000 litres of water a year — which means a significant hit to your Water Corporation bill, especially with Perth's tiered pricing. Smith Plumbing replaces worn washers, ceramic cartridges, and mixer valves across all tap brands and ages. We carry common parts on the van so most leaking tap jobs in Joondalup are fixed in a single visit, no callbacks needed.
