02 / Service

Blocked Drains

Joondalup's sandy soil and established tree canopy are notorious for causing root intrusion and sediment build-up in residential drains. Smith Plumbing uses high-pressure jet blasting and CCTV drain cameras to find the blockage fast and clear it properly — not just push it further down the line. Whether it's your kitchen sink, bathroom, or the main sewer drain out front, we'll have it flowing again the same day.

Gas Leaks

A gas leak is a serious safety emergency — if you smell gas in your Joondalup home, don't wait. Smith Plumbing is licensed for gas fitting work in WA and responds urgently to suspected leaks across the northern suburbs. We locate and repair the fault, pressure-test the lines, and give you a gas compliance certificate so you can feel safe again. Don't delay — call us immediately.

Leaking Taps

A dripping tap in Joondalup isn't just annoying — with Water Corporation restrictions and rising water bills, even a slow drip can waste thousands of litres a year. Smith Plumbing repairs and replaces all tap types including mixer taps, ball valves, quarter-turns, and outdoor garden taps. We carry common washer and cartridge sizes in the van, so most tap repairs are sorted in a single visit.
