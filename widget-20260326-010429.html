02 / SERVICE

Power Points & Switches

Running out of power points is a common headache in Perth's older Scarborough homes. We install additional GPOs, USB power points, and upgrade those ageing bakelite switches to modern slimline plates that look the part.

All work is fully compliant with AS/NZS 3000 wiring rules — no dodgy DIY here. We'll also check your existing circuits are rated for the load before adding new outlets.

03 / SERVICE

Safety Switch Installation

In Western Australia, safety switches (RCDs) are mandatory on all power and lighting circuits in new work — and retrofitting them to older Scarborough homes is one of the smartest things you can do for your family's safety.

Sparky's Electrical installs and tests safety switches to the latest WA regulations. Takes just an hour or two and the peace of mind is priceless. Don't wait until an incident makes it urgent.

04 / SERVICE

Smoke Alarm Installation

Western Australia's smoke alarm laws require hardwired photoelectric alarms in all residential properties. As of 2022, new requirements mean most Perth homes need interconnected alarms — meaning when one triggers, they all sound.

We supply and install compliant hardwired photoelectric smoke alarms throughout Scarborough and the northern suburbs. Whether you're selling, renting, or just want to be safe, we've got you covered with a certificate of compliance.

05 / SERVICE

LED Downlight Upgrades

Still running halogen downlights? You're burning money. Switching to quality LED downlights typically cuts lighting energy use by up to 75% — and with Perth's electricity costs climbing, that adds up fast.

We replace halogens with IC-rated, fire-compliant LED fittings and can install new downlights in any room. We'll also remove any old foam fire covers that halogens required — they're a fire risk with LEDs. Clean, fast, and affordable.

06 / SERVICE

Switchboard Upgrades

Older Scarborough homes often have ceramic fuse boards that simply can't handle the demands of modern appliances, reverse-cycle air con, or EV charging. An outdated switchboard is also a real insurance and safety issue.

We upgrade old fuse boards to modern circuit breaker switchboards with RCD protection, surge protection, and room for solar or EV circuits. Synergy-approved work, full compliance certificate provided as standard.
