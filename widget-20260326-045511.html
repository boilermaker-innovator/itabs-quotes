Perth Pro Plumbing —
Joondalup's trusted local plumbers

Family owned and operated since 2008, Perth Pro Plumbing has been keeping Joondalup and the northern suburbs flowing smoothly for over 15 years. Whether it's a dripping tap at 7am or a burst pipe on a Sunday afternoon, we treat every job with the same care and urgency we'd give our own home. No job too big or too small — just honest, friendly service from a team you can trust.

02 / Service

Blocked Drains

Blocked drains are one of the most common call-outs we handle across Joondalup, Currambine and Kingsley. Perth's sandy soils and tree-root intrusion from native gardens are frequent culprits — and left untreated, they can cause serious damage fast. We use high-pressure jet blasting and CCTV drain cameras to clear the blockage and find the cause, not just mask the symptoms.

03 / Service

Hot Water Systems

Waking up to cold water is never fun — especially on a chilly Joondalup winter morning. Perth Pro Plumbing supplies and installs all major hot water system brands, including Rheem, Rinnai and Dux. We handle gas, electric and heat pump units, and with WA rebates now available on heat pump systems, we'll make sure you're getting the best value for your home and budget.

04 / Service

Gas Leaks

A suspected gas leak is a serious emergency — don't wait. Our licensed gas plumbers respond fast across Joondalup and surrounding suburbs, locating and repairing leaks to keep your household safe. We're fully licensed under WA's Plumbers Licensing Board and work with both natural gas and LPG systems in homes and commercial properties. If you smell gas, call us immediately.

05 / Service

Burst Pipes

Burst pipes can flood your property in minutes. Our emergency plumbing team is on call to reach you fast across Joondalup, Edgewater, Beldon and the northern suburbs. Perth's high water pressure combined with ageing copper or poly pipework makes burst pipes more common than people expect — we'll locate the break, repair or replace the affected section, and check the rest of your system while we're there.

06 / Service

Bathroom Renovations

Thinking of giving your Joondalup home a bathroom refresh? We handle all the rough-in plumbing for new bathrooms and complete reno projects — from relocating waste lines to connecting new vanities, showers, toilets and baths. Working alongside tilers and builders across the northern suburbs, we make sure the plumbing side is done right the first time so your reno stays on schedule.
