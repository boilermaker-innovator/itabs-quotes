Why it gets bad fast
Perth summers are brutal. Warm weather and the occasional rain burst mean grass can grow several centimetres in a single week. Once it gets overgrown, you're looking at double the work — and more wear on mowing equipment. Staying on top of it with regular visits keeps the cost and effort low.
Southern suburbs Perth
Rick services suburbs including Fremantle, Cockburn, Rockingham, Kwinana, Hamilton Hill, Spearwood, Coolbellup, and surrounds. Local knowledge matters — Rick knows the soil types, the common grass varieties, and the seasonal quirks of properties in this part of Perth.

What's included in a mow?
A standard mow includes mowing the full lawn area, edging along paths, driveways and garden beds, and blowing down hard surfaces. Clippings are cleared as standard — Rick doesn't just mow and leave a mess behind.
Cleanup vs regular service
A one-off cleanup is perfect for properties that have been neglected or need a reset before going on a regular schedule. Regular maintenance keeps costs down — cleanups take more time and effort, so they're priced accordingly. Many customers start with a cleanup then move to fortnightly visits.
