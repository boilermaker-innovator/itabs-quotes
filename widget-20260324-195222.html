💡 Timber vs Composite

Timber gives that warm natural feel and is easy to repair. Composite is virtually maintenance-free and won't splinter — perfect for families with young kids or pets.

💰 03 / PRICING

What drives your deck cost in Perth?

Cost depends on size, material choice, and site conditions. Treated pine decks start from around $420/m², hardwood from $580/m², and composite from $640/m². Pergolas and carports are quoted per project. Ground-level builds cost less than elevated or sloped-site structures.

💡 The biggest cost driver

Site prep and subframe work often accounts for 30–40% of the total. A level block in Wanneroo is much cheaper to build on than a sloped garden in the hills.
