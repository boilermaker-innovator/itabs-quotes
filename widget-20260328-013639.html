02 / SERVICE

Blocked Drains

Joondalup's sandy soils and mature tree canopy mean root intrusion is one of the most common drain issues we see — especially in older homes around Edgewater and Beldon. Smith Plumbing uses high-pressure jet blasting and CCTV drain cameras to pinpoint and clear blockages fast, without unnecessary digging. From kitchen sinks to sewer mains, we'll have the water flowing again in no time.

03 / SERVICE

Hot Water Systems

Perth's water pressure and mineral content can be tough on hot water systems, and there's nothing worse than a cold shower on a Joondalup winter morning. We supply and install all major brands — Rheem, Rinnai, Dux, Aquamax — covering electric, gas, solar, and heat pump units. We'll help you choose the most energy-efficient option for your household size and budget, and handle the full installation including council paperwork.

04 / SERVICE

Gas Leaks

A gas leak is never something to ignore — if you smell gas in your Joondalup home, call us immediately. Smith Plumbing's licensed gasfitters respond quickly to diagnose and repair natural gas and LPG leaks throughout the northern suburbs. We use industry-standard pressure testing equipment to locate even the smallest leaks and carry out all repairs in full compliance with WA plumbing and gas regulations.

05 / SERVICE

Leaking Taps

With Perth's Water Corporation Stage 2 water restrictions in place year-round, a dripping tap isn't just annoying — it can waste thousands of litres a month and push up your Water Corp bill. Smith Plumbing fixes all types of tap leaks across Joondalup homes, from mixer taps and ceramic discs to pillar taps and outdoor hose bibs. We carry a wide range of parts so most repairs are completed in a single visit.
