About Blocked Drains

Blocked drains can result from tree roots, grease build-up, foreign objects or collapsed pipes. Pete uses CCTV inspection cameras and high-pressure water jetting to diagnose and clear blockages without unnecessary excavation. Price varies based on severity and access. Most jobs completed same day.

About Hot Water Systems

Hot water unit life expectancy is 8–12 years. Pete supplies and installs leading brands including Rheem, Rinnai and Vulcan. Prices vary by system type (electric, gas, heat pump) and tank size. Rebates may apply for heat pump upgrades. Call Pete for a same-day quote.

About Gas Fitting

All gas work in WA must be carried out by a licensed gas fitter. Pete holds full licensing and provides a Certificate of Compliance for every job. Services include new connections, appliance installation, leak detection and gas line extensions. Safety is always the priority.
