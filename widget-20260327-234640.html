02 / Service
Blocked Drains

Perth's sandy soils and tree root systems make blocked drains a common headache across Joondalup. We use electric eels and high-pressure water jetters to clear blockages fast — whether it's a slow kitchen sink, a backed-up bathroom or a main sewer line issue.

We also offer CCTV drain inspection so you know exactly what's going on underground before spending a dollar. Most Joondalup blocked drain jobs are resolved same day.

03 / Service
Hot Water Systems

When Joondalup mornings start cold and the shower runs icy, you need a plumber on-site fast. Smith Plumbing supplies and installs gas, electric, heat pump and solar hot water systems from leading brands like Rheem, Rinnai and Dux.

We handle everything from emergency replacements to planned upgrades. With WA government rebates available on heat pump and solar systems, we'll help you choose the most cost-effective option for your home and Joondalup's climate.

04 / Service
Gas Leaks

A gas leak is a serious emergency — if you smell rotten egg or hear a hissing pipe, don't delay. Smith Plumbing's licensed gas fitters respond quickly across Joondalup and the northern corridor, locating and repairing leaks to Plumbers Licensing Board WA standards.

We work on natural gas and LPG systems for both residential and light commercial properties — from faulty appliance connections and corroded pipework to full gas pressure testing and safety certificates. Your safety comes first, always.

05 / Service
Leaking Taps

A dripping tap in Joondalup can waste over 20,000 litres of water per year — and with Perth's water rates, that adds up fast. We fix leaking taps, mixers, shower heads and cisterns across all makes and models, sourcing genuine parts so the fix lasts.

We also handle full tapware upgrades and bathroom reconfigurations — perfect if you're renovating or simply want to modernise those tired 90s fittings. We're fully licensed and carry a wide range of parts on the van for same-visit repairs.
