Quick answers
Why it matters
An overgrown yard isn't just an eyesore — it can attract pests, create fire hazards in summer, and reduce your property value. Regular maintenance keeps your home looking sharp and your outdoor space usable all year round.
Perth summers
Perth summers are brutal. Grass can go from tidy to overgrown in a single week when the growing season kicks in. Having a regular mow schedule through spring and summer means you never fall behind — and your lawn stays healthy even in the heat.
