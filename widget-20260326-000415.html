Joondalup's trusted landscaping & bricklaying crew

From solid retaining walls to crisp brick edging, Rick's Trim & Brick has been shaping Joondalup backyards and streetscapes for years. We do the hard stuff properly — no shortcuts.

Whether you're in Joondalup, Edgewater, Beldon or anywhere in the northern suburbs, Rick and his team show up on time and deliver a finish you'll be proud of.

Rick's Trim & Brick is a family-run landscaping and bricklaying business based in Joondalup, specialising in retaining walls, feature paving, garden beds and brick edging across Perth's northern corridor. Rick brings years of hands-on trade experience to every job, personally overseeing the work to make sure standards stay high. The team is known for honest quotes, tidy worksites and results that hold up against Perth's summer heat and sandy soils.
02 / SERVICE

Retaining Walls

Perth's sandy soils and sloping blocks make retaining walls a must-have in many Joondalup properties. Rick's Trim & Brick builds walls in limestone, brick, concrete block and sleeper timber — all engineered to handle the load and comply with WA local council requirements.

Whether you need a low garden wall or a multi-tiered structure to reclaim a steep backyard, we design for drainage and longevity so your wall stays put — even after a wet Joondalup winter.

03 / SERVICE

Paving

A well-laid paved driveway, patio or path adds serious kerb appeal — and real value — to any Joondalup home. We work with clay brick, concrete pavers and natural stone, cutting and laying to your exact design.

Perth's UV and heat put pavers through their paces, so we use the right sub-base prep and jointing compounds to prevent shifting and cracking. Driveways, entertaining areas, pool surrounds — we handle it all.

04 / SERVICE

Brick Edging

Brick edging is the finishing touch that separates a tidy garden from a great one. Rick's team installs soldier course and on-edge brick borders that cleanly define lawns, garden beds and paths — keeping mulch in and creeping lawn out.

Popular across Joondalup and Currambine streetscapes, brick edging is low-maintenance and built to handle Perth's harsh clay and sandy soil conditions without heaving or cracking.
