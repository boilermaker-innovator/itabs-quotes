02 / Service
Timber Decking

A well-built timber deck transforms any Wanneroo backyard into a true outdoor living space — perfect for those long Perth summers when the family is outside from sunrise to sunset. Perth Decking Pros work with premium treated pine and Australian hardwood species including Jarrah and Merbau, chosen for their natural durability against WA's intense UV and termite exposure. Whether you want a wraparound entertainer's deck or a compact alfresco platform, they'll design and build it to last for decades.

03 / Service
Composite Decking

Composite decking is becoming the go-to choice for Perth homeowners who want the look of timber without the ongoing maintenance — no oiling, no splintering, and no fading in the relentless WA sun. Perth Decking Pros supply and install leading composite board systems that are slip-resistant and rated for Australia's harsh climate conditions, making them ideal for pool surrounds and coastal Wanneroo properties. It's a long-term investment that keeps looking great year after year with minimal effort.

04 / Service
Deck Repairs

Perth's sun, salt air, and termite pressure can take a real toll on older timber decks — boards split, posts rot, and fixings corrode over time. Perth Decking Pros offer thorough deck repair and restoration services across Wanneroo, assessing structural integrity, replacing damaged bearers and joists, and re-boarding worn surfaces to bring your deck back to its former glory. Don't demolish and replace when a professional repair can add another decade of life to your existing structure at a fraction of the cost.

05 / Service
Pergolas

A quality pergola creates shaded outdoor entertaining space that genuinely extends how you use your home — critical in Wanneroo where summer temperatures regularly hit the mid-40s. Perth Decking Pros design and build timber pergolas to complement your home's architecture, with options for louvred roofing, climbing plants, and integrated lighting to create the ultimate alfresco area. All structures are engineered to comply with WA building regulations and council approval requirements in the City of Wanneroo.

06 / Service
Carports

A timber carport not only protects your vehicles from Perth's brutal UV and occasional hail events but adds real value and street appeal to your Wanneroo property. Perth Decking Pros build freestanding and attached timber carports using treated structural pine and hardwood posts, designed to meet the City of Wanneroo's building code and cyclone wind-load requirements for the Perth metro zone. Every carport build includes a full design consultation and permit advice so there are no surprises along the way.
