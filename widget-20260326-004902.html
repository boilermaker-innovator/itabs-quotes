02 / Service

Blocked Drains

Perth's sandy soils and tree-root-heavy suburbs like Joondalup are notorious for causing stubborn drain blockages. We use high-pressure water jetting and CCTV drain cameras to locate and clear the problem fast — usually same day. Don't let a blocked drain turn into a sewage nightmare; call us early.

03 / Service

Hot Water Systems

Whether your existing unit has packed it in or you're switching from electric to gas to take advantage of Synergy tariff savings, we supply and install all leading hot water brands in Joondalup. We handle storage tanks, continuous flow and solar-boosted systems — fully compliant with WA energy regulations.

04 / Service

Gas Leaks

A gas leak is never something to wait on. Perth Pro Plumbing offers urgent gas leak detection and repair across Joondalup and the northern corridor — 7 days a week. We're licensed gas fitters who can isolate, test and repair your gas lines to full Atco and WA Gas Code compliance, keeping your family safe.

05 / Service

Burst Pipes

Perth's hot summer temperatures and shifting sandy ground put serious stress on older poly and copper pipes. When a pipe bursts, water damage spreads fast. Our team responds quickly across Joondalup and surrounds — we isolate the leak, make emergency repairs and restore your water supply with minimal disruption to your day.

06 / Service

Bathroom Renovations

Ready to upgrade your bathroom? Perth Pro Plumbing handles all the rough-in and fit-off plumbing for bathroom renovations across Joondalup — from a simple vanity and toilet swap to full wet area redesigns. We coordinate seamlessly with your tiler and builder, making the whole process stress-free from start to finish.
