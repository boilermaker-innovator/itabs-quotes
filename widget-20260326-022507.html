02 / Service

Colorbond Fencing

Colorbond is the go-to fencing choice across Perth's south, and for good reason — it handles the coastal salt air, summer heat and sandy conditions better than most alternatives. We stock the full BlueScope Colorbond palette so you can match your home or stand out on the street.

Our installs use galvanised steel posts set in concrete footings suited to the variable soils from Cockburn Sound through to Canning Vale. Standard and Infill paling profiles available — and we'll handle any City of Cockburn permit requirements for you.

03 / Service

Timber Fencing

Nothing adds character and warmth to a backyard like a well-built timber fence. We work with treated pine and hardwood palings to create everything from traditional lapped fences to contemporary feature designs that complement Perth's modern home styles.

In the south's sandy, moisture-variable soils, a good timber fence needs solid posts and quality treated timber — we don't cut corners. Popular across older suburbs like Hamilton Hill, Spearwood and Coolbellup where streetscape character matters. All timber treated for Perth's white ant belt.

04 / Service

Pool Fencing

Pool fencing in WA is strictly regulated and non-negotiable — every pool must meet Australian Standard AS1926 and be inspected by a registered building surveyor. We install glass panel, aluminium and frameless pool fencing that ticks every box and looks the part too.

We handle the compliance paperwork and liaise with the City of Cockburn or your local council inspection service on your behalf. Popular across new estates in Success, Atwell and Treeby where new pools are being installed in homes every week during Perth's building boom.

05 / Service

Gates & Access

A quality gate finishes off a fence job and adds real security and street appeal. We fabricate and install single and double driveway gates in Colorbond and steel, plus pedestrian gates in timber and aluminium to match your existing fence line.

We can fit manual or automated gate systems with keypad or remote access — a popular upgrade in the newer estates around Cockburn, Aubin Grove and Hammond Park. All gate hardware is heavy-duty and suited to Perth's sun-baked conditions where cheap hinges fail fast.
