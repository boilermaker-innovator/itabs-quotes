02 / Service

Colorbond Fencing

Colorbond is the go-to choice for Perth homeowners — it handles our harsh summer heat, salt air from the coast, and strong Fremantle Doctor winds without fading or warping. Perth Fencing Co installs Colorbond boundary fencing across Osborne Park, Stirling and surrounding suburbs, using only BlueScope Steel product for lasting durability. Pricing typically starts from $85–$120 per metre depending on height and site conditions.

03 / Service

Timber Fencing

A quality timber fence adds natural warmth and character to any Osborne Park property. We work with treated pine and hardwood options suited to Perth's climate, properly treated to resist borers and moisture. Whether you're after a privacy fence, picket fence or feature boundary, we'll build it to a standard that genuinely lasts. Expect to invest around $70–$100 per metre for a professionally installed timber fence.

04 / Service

Pool Fencing

Pool fencing in Western Australia is governed by strict Building Commission regulations — all pools must be fully compliant with AS 1926.1. Perth Fencing Co handles the full installation process, ensuring your glass or aluminium pool fence meets every requirement so you can enjoy your pool without any compliance headaches. We service Osborne Park, Innaloo, Karrinyup and across the City of Stirling. Pool fencing typically ranges from $120–$180 per metre fully installed.

05 / Service

Retaining Walls

Perth's sandy soils and sloping blocks — particularly common across the northern suburbs — make a solid retaining wall essential for protecting your property and landscaping. Perth Fencing Co builds retaining walls in treated timber, limestone block and concrete sleeper, engineered to handle soil pressure and Perth's dry summers. Walls over 500mm typically require council approval and we can advise you through the process. Pricing from $280–$450 per metre depending on height and material.

06 / Service

Gate Installation

A gate that doesn't swing true or closes poorly is a constant frustration. Perth Fencing Co installs pedestrian gates, double driveway gates and automated sliding gates to match your existing or new fencing perfectly. We use heavy-duty hinges and hardware rated for Perth's heat and UV, so your gate keeps working smoothly year after year. Pedestrian gates start from $450, with double driveway gates from $750–$900 supply and install.
