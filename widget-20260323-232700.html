🔩 02 / TYPES

Colorbond, timber or pool fencing — we do it all

Colorbond is Perth's most popular choice — tough, low-maintenance and available in 22 colours to match your home. Timber adds a warm, natural feel perfect for garden spaces and rear yards. Pool fencing is non-negotiable for safety compliance, and we install both aluminium and glass frameless options to suit any backyard in the southern suburbs.

💡 Think of it like...
Choosing a fence is like choosing a car — steel (Colorbond) is reliable and long-lasting, timber is classic and warm, and glass/aluminium is the prestige option for pools.
💰 03 / PRICING

What actually drives your fencing cost

The biggest cost factors are fence type, lineal metres needed, and site conditions. A rocky limestone block in Baldivis will cost more to post than flat sandy soil in Success. Gate additions, post upgrades and removing your old fence also add to the total. We price fairly with no hidden surprises on invoice day.

💡 Think of it like...
It's like buying flooring — the material rate per metre is only part of the cost. Prep, access, and removing the old stuff is where jobs vary most.
📋 04 / PROCESS

From quote to finished fence in days, not weeks

Step one is a free on-site measure and quote — we check soil type, access, existing footings and your neighbour situation. Once approved, materials are ordered and most residential jobs in Cockburn, Fremantle or Rockingham are completed in 1–2 days. We handle all Dial Before You Dig checks and clean up completely when we're done.

💡 Think of it like...
Think of it like a kitchen reno — the planning and measurement phase is everything. Get that right and the installation day runs like clockwork.
