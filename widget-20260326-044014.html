Colorbond Fencing

Colorbond is the go-to fencing choice for Perth homes — and for good reason. It handles our blazing summers, coastal winds and sandy soils with ease. Perth Fencing Co installs Colorbond in all standard colours, from Surfmist to Woodland Grey, with full-height and slat styles to suit your property and council requirements.

🏗️ From $85–$120 per metre installed

Timber Fencing

Timber fencing adds warmth and natural character to any Perth property. We use quality treated pine and hardwood options that are treated to resist termites, rot and the dry Perth heat. Whether you're after a classic picket fence for a Subiaco cottage or a solid privacy screen for your Osborne Park home, we build it to last.

🌲 From $70–$100 per metre installed
04 / Service

Aluminium Fencing

Aluminium slat fencing is increasingly popular across Perth's northern suburbs for its sleek, modern look and virtually zero maintenance. It won't rust, warp or peel — perfect for Perth's salt-laden coastal air in nearby suburbs like Scarborough and Trigg. Available in a full range of powder-coat colours to match your home's façade.

✨ Sleek, modern & low-maintenance
05 / Service

Pool Fencing

Pool fencing in WA must comply with strict Building Code of Australia and Council of Australian Governments (COAG) safety standards — and Perth Fencing Co knows every requirement inside out. We supply and install frameless glass, semi-frameless and aluminium pool fencing that's fully compliant, beautifully finished and inspected to pass first time.

🏊 From $120–$180 per metre installed
06 / Service

Gate Installation

A quality gate completes your fence and secures your property. Perth Fencing Co installs pedestrian gates, driveway gates and automated sliding gates across Osborne Park and the wider Perth metro area. All gates are custom fabricated to fit your opening precisely, with matching finishes to your existing or new fencing.

🚪 From $450–$900 supplied & installed
