02 / SERVICE

Blocked Drains

Joondalup's sandy soils and mature street trees are notorious for root intrusion into drainage lines — and it usually happens at the worst possible moment. Smith Plumbing uses high-pressure jetting and CCTV inspection to clear blockages fast and diagnose what caused them in the first place. Most residential blockages are fully resolved on the same visit.

03 / SERVICE

Hot Water Systems

Perth's hard water is tough on hot water systems — scale build-up and anode rod corrosion cut years off the life of your unit. Whether your electric, gas, or solar hot water system has failed completely or you're ready to upgrade to a heat pump or instant gas system, Smith Plumbing handles supply and installation across Joondalup and the northern suburbs. We stock the leading brands and can often replace same-day.

04 / SERVICE

Gas Leaks

A gas leak is never something to sit on — if you can smell gas at your Joondalup property, turn off the supply at the meter and call Smith Plumbing immediately. We're fully licensed for Type A gas fitting work in WA and respond rapidly to suspected leaks, faulty gas appliance connections, and new gas line installations. Don't risk it — safety first, every time.

05 / SERVICE

Leaking Taps

A single dripping tap can waste over 20,000 litres of water a year — and with Perth Water Corp's tier pricing, that drip is costing you real money. Smith Plumbing handles tap re-washering, ceramic cartridge replacement, and full mixer tap replacements across Joondalup. We carry common washer and cartridge sizes on the van so there's usually no need for a return visit.
