02 / SERVICE
Blocked Drains

Joondalup's coastal soils and mature tree canopies mean root intrusion and debris blockages are incredibly common. Smith Plumbing uses high-pressure water jetting and CCTV drain cameras to clear and diagnose blockages fast — from kitchen sinks to main sewer lines.

We're typically on-site the same day, and most residential blocked drains are resolved in a single visit. Typical cost: $180–$320.

03 / SERVICE
Hot Water Systems

Perth's hard water is notorious for cutting hot water system lifespans short. Whether your storage tank has failed or you're ready to upgrade to an energy-efficient heat pump or continuous gas system, Smith Plumbing handles supply and installation across all major brands.

We're familiar with WA's rebate schemes for solar and heat pump hot water — we'll help you choose the right system and maximise any eligible savings. Typical cost: $900–$1,800.

04 / SERVICE
Gas Leaks

A gas leak is a plumbing emergency — don't wait. Smith Plumbing are fully licensed gas fitters in WA, able to respond urgently to suspected leaks, faulty gas appliances, and gas line repairs across Joondalup and surrounding suburbs.

If you smell gas, leave the property, don't operate switches, and call us immediately. We carry gas detection equipment on every van and can isolate, locate, and repair leaks safely and to code. Typical inspection & repair: $180–$350.

05 / SERVICE
Leaking Taps

A dripping tap in Perth wastes thousands of litres of our precious water every year — and adds up on your Water Corporation bill. Smith Plumbing replaces washers, O-rings, ceramic cartridges, and tap seats quickly and affordably across all tap types and brands.

We also check your water pressure at the same visit, as Joondalup's mains pressure can be high enough to cause ongoing tap wear if left unmanaged. Typical cost: $180–$280 per tap.
