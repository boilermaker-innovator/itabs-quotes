02 / SERVICE
Interior Painting

Transform your home's interior with a professional finish that lasts. We prep every surface properly — filling cracks, sanding, priming — before laying down premium low-VOC paints suited to Fremantle's coastal humidity. Whether it's a single room refresh or a full house repaint, the results speak for themselves.

03 / SERVICE
Exterior Painting

Perth's UV index and Fremantle's ocean air are brutal on exterior paint. We use only top-grade exterior coatings with UV inhibitors and salt-air resistance built in — the kind that won't chalk, peel or fade within a few summers. Proper surface prep including pressure washing, gap filling and priming is always included.

04 / SERVICE
Roof Painting

A freshly painted roof doesn't just look great — it protects your home and can reflect heat to reduce energy bills during Perth's scorching summers. We pressure-wash, treat any lichen or mould, apply a quality primer and finish with a durable membrane-grade roof paint. Ideal for Fremantle's ageing terracotta and Colorbond rooftops.

05 / SERVICE
Deck & Fence Staining

Timber decks and fences in Perth take a real beating from the sun and sea breeze. We sand back weathered timber, clean out any mould or grey oxidation, and apply a penetrating stain or quality paint system that soaks in deep and protects for years. Great for Freo's classic jarrah decks and Colorbond fences alike.

06 / SERVICE
Commercial Painting

From Fremantle's iconic café strips and heritage storefronts to offices and retail fitouts across the Perth metro, Top Cut Painting delivers commercial work on schedule, within budget and with minimal disruption to your business. We're fully insured for commercial projects and happy to work after hours or on weekends.
