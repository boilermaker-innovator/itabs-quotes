Between work, family, and everything else, the lawn is always the last thing on the list. Before you know it, you've got knee-high kikuyu and a bin full of excuses. Regular maintenance stops the problem before it starts — and costs a lot less than a full rescue job.

Perth's warm climate means grass and weeds grow fast through spring and summer, often requiring a mow every 2–3 weeks. Even in winter, kikuyu and couch grass keep creeping. Without a regular schedule, things spiral quickly in the southern suburbs.
