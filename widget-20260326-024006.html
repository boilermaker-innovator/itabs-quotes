02 / Service
Blocked Drain Clearing
Blocked drains are one of the most common plumbing calls across Osborne Park and nearby suburbs like Stirling, Innaloo and Wembley. Perth's clay-heavy soils and prolific tree roots — think Moreton Bays and liquid ambers — are notorious for infiltrating stormwater and sewer lines. Perth Metro Plumbing uses high-pressure jet blasting and CCTV camera inspection to locate and clear blockages fast, so you're not left with an overflowing toilet or flooded laundry.
03 / Service
Hot Water Systems
Perth's hard water and high UV environment can shorten the life of hot water systems, making replacements more common than the manufacturer's warranty suggests. Perth Metro Plumbing supplies and installs gas, electric, heat pump and solar hot water units — and can advise on the WA government rebates available for heat pump upgrades. Emergency same-day replacements are available across the Osborne Park area when your system fails without warning.
04 / Service
Leak Detection & Repairs
With Water Corporation water restrictions and Perth's rising water bills, even a small leak can cost you hundreds of dollars a year without you knowing. Perth Metro Plumbing uses acoustic leak detection and thermal imaging to pinpoint leaks hidden behind walls or under slabs — common in Osborne Park's mix of 1970s brick-and-tile homes and newer builds. Repairs are quoted upfront with no surprise callout markups.
05 / Service
Fixture & Tapware Installation
Upgrading tapware, toilets, basins or laundry fixtures is a cost-effective way to modernise an Osborne Park home without a full renovation. Perth Metro Plumbing handles supply-and-install or supply-your-own for all major brands stocked at nearby Reece and Tradelink. All installations meet WA plumbing regulations and come with a workmanship guarantee — so your new Caroma or Methven fittings are fitted correctly the first time.
06 / Service
Gas Fitting & Appliances
Natural gas is available across most of the Osborne Park corridor, and Perth Metro Plumbing's gas-licensed plumbers can connect or service BBQs, heaters, cooktops and gas hot water systems. All gas work is pressure-tested and compliant with WA Energy Safety regulations. Whether you're adding a new gas point for an outdoor kitchen or replacing an ageing gas heater before winter, the team can scope the job and quote on the spot.
