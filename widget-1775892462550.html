01 / THE PROBLEM
Your lawn is out of control again
It starts with missing one mow. Then two. Before you know it, the grass is knee-high, the hedges are scratching the fence, and weeds have taken over the garden beds. Sound familiar? You're not alone — it happens to thousands of Perth homeowners every season.
Think of it like...
Skipping the gym for a month. Getting back on track takes twice the effort. The same goes for your lawn — a quick regular visit beats a massive rescue job every time.
Why it escalates
Grass that's left too long becomes harder to cut cleanly. It stresses the lawn, kills patches, and creates the perfect hiding spot for pests and weeds. Each week you wait adds more work — and more cost — to get it back to a healthy state.
Perth summers
In Perth's southern suburbs, the summer heat can turn an overgrown lawn dry and patchy within days. Regular mowing at the right height actually helps grass retain moisture and stay greener longer — even through the hottest months.
