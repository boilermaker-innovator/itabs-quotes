Rick's Trim & Brick — Joondalup's trusted bricklaying & landscaping crew

Rick's Trim & Brick has been shaping yards across Joondalup and the northern suburbs for years — from solid retaining walls that handle Perth's sandy soils, to crisp brick edging and custom garden beds that actually last. Rick and his team are local, licenced, and don't cut corners on materials or craft.

Based in Joondalup, Rick's Trim & Brick specialises in landscaping and bricklaying work that's built to suit Perth's unique conditions — sandy substrate, hot summers, and coastal winds. Rick handles every quote personally and uses quality materials sourced locally. Whether you need a retaining wall to stop soil creep or a full garden redesign with paving and brick edging, Rick's team delivers tidy work with a no-fuss attitude.
02 / Service

Retaining Walls

Perth's sandy soils shift and erode, especially on sloped blocks — a well-built retaining wall is essential for anyone in the Joondalup hills or lakeside estates. Rick builds brick and limestone retaining walls engineered to stay level and true through summers and wet winters alike. All walls over 500mm comply with WA building regulations, and Rick handles the paperwork.

03 / Service

Paving

From driveways and alfresco areas to pool surrounds and pathways, Rick's Trim & Brick lays clay brick, concrete, and limestone pavers across Joondalup and the City of Wanneroo. Perth's hot summers demand proper base prep and jointing compound that won't crack or wash away — that's exactly how Rick's team does it every time.

04 / Service

Brick Edging

Brick edging is the finishing touch that makes a Joondalup garden look sharp and professional — it keeps lawns, mulch beds and gravel zones neatly defined even after a good downpour. Rick uses soldier-course brick and half-brick edging styles set on a solid mortar bed so they stay crisp season after season. Perfect for new builds across the northern corridor.

05 / Service

Garden Beds

Raised brick garden beds add structure, drainage and style to any Joondalup yard — especially useful in Perth's sandy soil where water runs straight through without a proper build-up of mix and organic material. Rick designs and constructs custom garden beds in brick, limestone or block, filled with premium soil blends so your plants actually thrive from day one.
