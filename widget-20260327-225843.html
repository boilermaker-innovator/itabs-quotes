Smith Plumbing has been keeping Joondalup homes and businesses flowing smoothly for years. Whether it's an urgent blocked drain, a failing hot water system, or a gas leak that can't wait — we show up fast, fix it right, and leave the place cleaner than we found it. Local knowledge, honest pricing, no call-out surprises.
02 / Service
Blocked Drains
Joondalup's older suburbs are notorious for tree root intrusion in ageing clay sewer lines — Smith Plumbing handles it all with high-pressure water jetting and CCTV drain cameras. We clear kitchen sinks, bathroom drains, stormwater systems and main sewers fast, with same-day availability for urgent blockages. No drainage job is too messy for our crew.
03 / Service
Hot Water Systems
Perth's hard water is tough on hot water systems, and when yours gives out in winter it's no fun. Smith Plumbing supplies and installs electric, gas, heat pump and solar hot water units across Joondalup and the northern suburbs. We stock leading brands and can often replace your unit the same day you call — no cold showers, no waiting weeks for parts.
04 / Service
Gas Leaks
A gas leak is always an emergency — and Smith Plumbing is licenced and equipped to respond fast across Joondalup. We use electronic gas detectors to pinpoint leaks in supply lines, appliance connections and metres, then repair or replace the affected section on the spot. We're fully compliant with WA gas safety regulations and work with Alinta Energy for meter isolation when required.
05 / Service
Leaking Taps
A dripping tap wastes thousands of litres of Perth's precious water every year and will send your Water Corporation bill through the roof. Smith Plumbing regrinding taps, replacing washers, ceramic cartridges and entire mixer sets for kitchens, bathrooms and laundries across Joondalup. Quick fix visits are affordable and we carry most parts on the van — usually done in one visit.
