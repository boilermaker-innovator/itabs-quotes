02 / Service
Power Points
& Switches

Running out of power points is one of Perth's most common household headaches — especially as home offices and EV chargers become the norm in northern suburbs like Scarborough and Karrinyup. Sparky's installs single, double and USB power points quickly and cleanly, with zero mess left behind.

03 / Service
Safety Switch
Installation

WA regulations require safety switches (RCDs) on all power and lighting circuits in homes — and many older Scarborough and Doubleview properties are still not fully compliant. Sparky's Electrical will assess your switchboard, install the correct safety switches, and bring your home up to current AS/NZS standards, keeping your family protected.

04 / Service
Smoke Alarm
Installation

WA's updated smoke alarm laws now require photoelectric, interconnected alarms in all Perth homes — particularly important for properties being sold or rented in Scarborough. Sparky's Electrical installs hardwired photoelectric smoke alarms that meet the latest regulations, ensuring your property is safe, compliant, and insurable.

05 / Service
LED Downlight
Upgrades

Switching from old halogen downlights to modern LED fittings can cut your lighting energy costs by up to 75% — a real win with Perth's summer heat and cooling bills. Sparky's upgrades your halogens to quality LED downlights in a single tidy visit, with consistent colour temperature and proper fire-rated fittings throughout your home.

06 / Service
Switchboard
Upgrades

Many older homes in Scarborough and surrounding northern suburbs still run ageing ceramic fuse boards that can't safely handle modern appliances, air conditioning or solar systems. Sparky's Electrical upgrades your switchboard to a modern safety-switch-equipped consumer mains unit — essential if you're adding solar, a home battery, or an EV charger.
