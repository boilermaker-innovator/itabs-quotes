01 / Rick's Trim & Brick
Rick's Trim & Brick —
Joondalup's go-to crew for walls, paving & garden builds
Rick's Trim & Brick has been transforming backyards and front gardens across Joondalup and the northern suburbs for years. From solid brick retaining walls to pristine paved driveways, Rick and his team know Perth's sandy soils, limestone conditions and council setbacks inside out. If you want it built right and looking sharp, this is your crew.
02 / Service
Retaining Walls
Perth's sandy, shifting soils make a quality retaining wall essential for anyone with a sloped block in Joondalup. Rick's team builds brick and limestone retaining walls that are engineered to last, whether you need a low garden border or a fully engineered wall over 1.0m that meets WA council requirements. Every wall is finished cleanly and built to hold.
03 / Service
Paving
From exposed aggregate driveways to limestone-look clay paving around your pool, Rick's Trim & Brick handles it all across Joondalup and surrounds. With Perth's hot summers and UV exposure, Rick uses quality materials and proper bedding sand prep so your paving stays level and weed-free for the long haul. Driveways, alfresco areas, pathways — done properly the first time.
04 / Service
Brick Edging
Neat brick edging is the difference between a garden that looks thrown together and one that looks cared for. Rick's crew lays soldier-course brick borders and mowing strips throughout Joondalup that keep your lawn, garden beds and paths cleanly separated. It's a small job that makes a massive difference to your kerb appeal and makes mowing a breeze.
05 / Service
Garden Beds
Raised brick garden beds are a popular choice across Joondalup's coastal-climate blocks — they look great, improve drainage in Perth's sandy soils and create a defined space for native plants and low-water landscaping. Rick builds raised garden beds in brick, limestone or block to any height and shape, with quality backfill and mulch included to get your plants thriving from day one.
