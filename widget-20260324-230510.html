What drives your fencing cost in Perth

Fencing is priced per linear metre, so your total depends on fence length, chosen material, height, and any site challenges like sloping ground or rock. Perth prices typically range from $70–$185 per metre installed.

Gates, corner posts, removal of existing fencing, and council application fees are common add-ons. Our free quote covers everything so there are no surprises on invoice day.
