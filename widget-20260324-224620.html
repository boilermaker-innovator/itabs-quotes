Treated pine is budget-friendly and great for painting. Hardwood like merbau is naturally durable and looks stunning natural. Composite never needs oiling or staining — ever.

💰 03 / PRICING

What drives the cost of your deck

Deck pricing in Perth depends on four main factors: size (square metres), timber or composite choice, site access and height, and any added features like pergola cover or built-in seating. Ground-level treated pine decks start from around $420/m², while composite or elevated builds run $580–$750/m².

💡 Size Matters Most

A 20m² deck is your weekend BBQ spot. A 40m² deck is a full outdoor room. Every extra square metre costs less per unit — so going bigger often makes better value.
