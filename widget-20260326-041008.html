02 / Service

Colorbond Fencing

Colorbond is Perth's most popular fencing choice — and for good reason. It handles the harsh WA sun without warping, fading or rusting, and comes in a wide range of colours to suit any Osborne Park home. Perth Fencing Co installs full Colorbond panels, retaining fence combinations and feature sections, all compliant with local council setback requirements.

Pricing from $85–$120 per metre, fully installed with concrete footings.

03 / Service

Timber Fencing

A classic timber paling fence adds natural warmth and character that suits Perth's older character suburbs and new estates alike. We use premium hardwood and treated pine that's matched to Perth's climate — resistant to termites and UV degradation. Whether you want a lapped-and-capped privacy fence or a traditional paling boundary, we'll deliver it built to last.

Pricing from $70–$100 per metre installed, with staining and sealing options available.

04 / Service

Aluminium Fencing

Aluminium fencing gives Osborne Park properties a sleek, contemporary look with zero rust, zero rot and minimal maintenance. It's especially popular for front boundary fences, sloped properties and decorative screening in the Perth metro area. Our aluminium installations are powder-coated for long-term colour retention in the WA sun.

Pricing from $95–$145 per metre depending on panel style and height.

05 / Service

Pool Fencing

Pool fencing in WA must comply with strict Building Commission regulations — and Perth Fencing Co knows every requirement inside out. We install frameless glass, semi-frameless glass and aluminium pool fencing that meets all Perth council codes, with self-closing latches and compliant gate systems. We also handle pool fence compliance inspections and repairs.

Pricing from $120–$180 per metre — gates from $450–$900. Free compliance assessment included.

Gate Installation

A quality gate completes your fence and adds serious kerb appeal. Perth Fencing Co installs pedestrian gates, driveway gates and automated sliding or swing gates across Osborne Park and surrounding suburbs. We match gate styles to your existing fencing — Colorbond, timber, aluminium or pool — for a seamless, professional finish.

Gates from $450 for pedestrian up to $2,800+ for automated driveway gates, fully installed.
