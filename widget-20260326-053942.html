02 / SERVICE
Colorbond fencing built for Perth's coastal climate

Colorbond is the go-to choice for Rockingham homes — it handles the salt air, summer heat and coastal winds that chew through cheaper materials fast. We supply and install Colorbond steel fencing in the full BlueScope range, and our posts are set properly in concrete to APRA standards so they won't lean or shift after the first big storm. Whether you're replacing an ageing timber fence or putting up a new boundary on a new build, we'll get it done neatly and on schedule.

03 / SERVICE
Timber fencing with a clean, classic finish

Timber fencing adds warmth and character that Colorbond simply can't match — and done right, it holds up well even in the Rockingham coastal zone. We use treated pine and hardwood options suited to WA conditions, treating every post base to resist moisture and termite activity. A timber fence from Pete's team is properly aligned, neatly capped and won't look saggy or patchy a year down the track.

04 / SERVICE
Pool fencing that meets WA compliance, no stress

Pool fencing in WA is regulated under the Building Act and must comply with AS 1926.1 — get it wrong and your pool inspection will fail, delaying your certificate of occupancy or putting you at legal risk. Pete's team installs frameless glass and aluminium pool fencing that meets all City of Rockingham and Shire of Kwinana requirements. We handle the whole job from measure-up to final check, so you can enjoy your pool without the compliance headache.

05 / SERVICE
Gate installation — sliding, swing and driveway

A quality gate finishes off any fence line and improves security and street appeal for your Rockingham property. We install Colorbond swing gates, sliding driveway gates and pedestrian access gates — all hung level on solid posts with quality hardware that won't rust out in a year. If you're after automated driveway gates, we work alongside a trusted local auto gate supplier to get the motor installed and running smoothly.
