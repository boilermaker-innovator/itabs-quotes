02 / SERVICE

Blocked Drains

Joondalup's sandy soils and mature tree roots are a nightmare combo for residential drains — and we see it every week. Smith Plumbing uses high-pressure jet blasting and CCTV camera inspection to locate and clear blockages fast, without tearing up your garden. Most blocked drain jobs are resolved same day.

03 / SERVICE

Hot Water Systems

Perth's hard water can wreak havoc on hot water systems, especially older storage tanks in the Joondalup area. Whether your system has failed completely or you're ready to upgrade to a more energy-efficient heat pump or instant gas unit, Smith Plumbing supplies and installs all major brands with same-day availability on most jobs.

04 / SERVICE

Gas Leaks

Smell gas at your Joondalup property? Don't wait — Smith Plumbing offers emergency gas leak detection and repair 7 days a week. As licensed gas fitters, we work safely and in full compliance with WA gas regulations. We'll locate the leak, make it safe, and repair or replace the faulty line or appliance connection on the spot.

05 / SERVICE

Leaking Taps

A dripping tap in Joondalup isn't just annoying — with Perth's water restrictions and rising costs, it can add hundreds of dollars to your water bill each year. Smith Plumbing repairs or replaces worn washers, ceramic discs, and tap fittings across all brands quickly and affordably. We also carry common tap sets for a same-visit upgrade if you're ready for a freshen up.
