Smith Plumbing — Perth's trusted local plumber

Smith Plumbing has been keeping Perth homes and businesses flowing smoothly for years. Whether it's a blocked drain ruining your morning or a hot water system that's packed it in overnight, we're on call and ready to get the job done right — the first time.

Based right here in Perth, we understand the local plumbing landscape — from older homes in the inner suburbs with cast iron pipes to new estates in the outer metro dealing with high water pressure. We're fully licensed, insured, and genuinely reliable.

02 / Service

Blocked Drains cleared fast

Perth's older suburbs — Subiaco, Fremantle, Victoria Park — are notorious for tree root intrusion into ageing clay sewer lines. Smith Plumbing uses high-pressure water jetting and CCTV drain cameras to locate and clear blockages quickly and without unnecessary excavation.

From a slow kitchen sink to a completely blocked sewer, we carry the equipment to handle it on the spot. Most blocked drain jobs are resolved in a single visit, and we'll give you an honest assessment of whether your pipes need any further attention.

03 / Service

Hot Water Systems repaired & replaced

Waking up to cold water in a Perth winter morning is never fun. Smith Plumbing services, repairs, and installs all major hot water system brands — electric, gas, solar, and heat pump. We stock parts for Rheem, Dux, Rinnai, and Bosch so most repairs can be done on the first visit.

If your system is beyond repair, we'll walk you through the most cost-effective replacement options for your household size and energy setup. Given WA's solar potential, a heat pump or solar hot water upgrade can pay for itself in just a few years.

04 / Service

Gas Leaks — emergency response

Gas leaks are a serious safety hazard and must never be ignored. Smith Plumbing holds a full gas fitting licence as required by the WA Department of Energy, Mines, Industry Regulation and Safety. If you smell gas — rotten eggs or sulphur — call us immediately.

We respond quickly to gas leak calls across Perth metro, using electronic gas detectors to pinpoint leaks in pipework, appliances, and fittings. We'll isolate the problem safely, carry out compliant repairs, and restore your gas supply with a full pressure test and sign-off.

05 / Service

Leaking Taps fixed right

A single dripping tap can waste over 9,000 litres of water a year — a big deal in Perth where water restrictions are part of life. Smith Plumbing repairs and replaces all tap types — ceramic disc, ball valves, mixer taps, and old quarter-turn washers — quickly and affordably.

We carry a wide range of replacement parts for common Australian brands like Caroma, Methven, and Tradelink so there's no waiting around for parts. We'll also check for any hidden moisture damage behind the wall while we're there, giving you peace of mind the fix is complete.
