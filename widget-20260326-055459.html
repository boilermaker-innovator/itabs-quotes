Pete's Fencing Co — Rockingham's trusted fencing crew

Pete's Fencing Co has been building and replacing fences across Rockingham and the southern Perth suburbs for years. Whether you need a solid Colorbond boundary fence, a coastal-grade timber job that handles the sea breeze, or a compliant pool fence to satisfy council, Pete and his team get it done properly the first time.

02 / SERVICE

Colorbond Fencing

Colorbond is the go-to fencing choice across Rockingham — it handles the coastal salt air and harsh WA summer sun better than almost anything else. Pete installs genuine BlueScope steel Colorbond in a full range of colours to match your home, with proper concrete footing on every post for long-term stability. Perfect for boundary fences, side runs, and privacy screens throughout the Rockingham area.

03 / SERVICE

Timber Fencing

A well-built timber fence adds natural warmth and great street appeal to any Rockingham home. Pete uses treated hardwood or pine rails and palings suited to coastal conditions, with termite-treated posts set in concrete. Timber is ideal for garden borders, front boundary fences, and properties where character matters as much as function.

04 / SERVICE

Pool Fencing

Pool fencing in WA is mandatory and must meet strict Australian Standards (AS 1926) — Pete knows the Rockingham council requirements inside and out and installs compliant frameless glass, semi-frameless, or aluminium pool fencing. Every job is measured and built to pass inspection first time, giving you peace of mind and keeping your family safe.

05 / SERVICE

Gate Installation

A solid, well-hung gate pulls your whole fence together — Pete supplies and installs single and double driveway gates, pedestrian access gates, and automated sliding gates to suit Colorbond, timber, or pool fencing. All gates are properly braced and fitted with quality latches and hinges rated for the coastal Rockingham climate so they swing true for years.
