Smith Plumbing — Joondalup's trusted local plumber

Fast, reliable plumbing for homes across Joondalup and the northern suburbs. From blocked drains to full hot water system replacements, we show up on time and get the job done right.

02 / SERVICE

Blocked drains cleared fast

Joondalup's sandy soil and mature tree roots are notorious for cracking and blocking residential drain pipes. Smith Plumbing uses high-pressure jet blasting and CCTV drain cameras to locate and clear blockages without unnecessary digging. Same-day service available across Joondalup, Edgewater, and Currambine.

💧 Jet blasting 📷 CCTV camera ⚡ Same day
03 / SERVICE

Hot water systems supplied & installed

Whether your old storage tank has given up the ghost or you're upgrading to an energy-efficient heat pump, Smith Plumbing handles the full supply and installation. We work with Rheem, Rinnai, and Dux systems and are fully compliant with WA plumbing standards. Most Joondalup installs completed the same day you call.

🔥 Gas & electric ⚡ Same day 🏷️ All brands
04 / SERVICE

Gas leaks — emergency response

A gas leak in your Joondalup home is a serious safety emergency. Smith Plumbing's licensed gas fitters are on call for urgent leak detection, isolation, and repair. We carry electronic gas detectors and can make your property safe, then carry out a full pressure test and compliance inspection before reconnecting your supply.

🚨 Emergency 🔍 Leak detection ✅ Licensed
05 / SERVICE

Leaking taps & fixtures repaired

A dripping tap wastes thousands of litres a year — and with Perth's water restrictions, that's money straight down the drain. Smith Plumbing repairs and replaces all tap types including mixer taps, ball valves, and quarter-turn ceramic cartridges. We carry a wide range of Caroma and Methven spares for quick turnaround across Joondalup.

💧 All tap types 🔧 Same-day parts 💰 Fixed pricing
