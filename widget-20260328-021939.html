02 / Service
Blocked Drains — cleared fast

Perth's sandy soils and mature street trees make blocked drains one of the most common plumbing issues in the metro area — especially in older suburbs where clay pipes are still in the ground. Smith Plumbing uses high-pressure jet blasting and CCTV camera inspections to clear and diagnose blockages quickly, whether it's a kitchen sink in Mount Lawley or a sewer line in Canning Vale. Most blocked drains are resolved in a single visit.

03 / Service
Hot Water Systems — supply & install

Perth's year-round sunshine makes solar and heat pump hot water systems incredibly popular — and with rising energy costs, upgrading your old electric storage unit can save serious money on your power bill. Smith Plumbing supplies and installs all major brands including Rheem, Dux, Rinnai and Aquamax, and we handle the STC rebate paperwork so you don't have to. Emergency same-day replacements are available across the Perth metro.

04 / Service
Gas Leaks — safe, licensed response

A gas leak in your Perth home is a serious safety issue and must only be handled by a licensed gas fitter — never attempt a DIY repair. Smith Plumbing holds all required WA gas fitting licences and can respond urgently to gas odours, faulty connections and corroded lines across the Perth metro area. We carry gas detection equipment and can isolate, repair and pressure-test your gas lines to meet AS/NZS standards before turning supply back on.

05 / Service
Leaking Taps — stop wasting water

With Perth's strict Water Corporation restrictions, a dripping tap isn't just annoying — it can cost you in fines and wasted water. A single leaking tap can waste up to 2,000 litres per month. Smith Plumbing replaces washers, O-rings, ceramic discs and full tap fixtures across all brands and styles, from heritage tapware in Cottesloe to modern mixers in the newer estates of Joondalup. Quick, affordable and guaranteed.
