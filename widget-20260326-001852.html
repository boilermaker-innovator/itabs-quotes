01 / Rick's Trim & Brick
Rick's Trim & Brick — Joondalup's trusted landscaping & bricklaying crew

Rick's Trim & Brick has been transforming backyards and front gardens across Joondalup and Perth's northern suburbs for years. From solid retaining walls on Joondalup's sloped blocks to sharp brick edging that keeps garden beds tidy through the hot WA summers, Rick and his team do it all properly, the first time.

Rick's Trim & Brick is a family-run landscaping and bricklaying business based in Joondalup, serving the northern suburbs of Perth. Rick prides himself on clean, long-lasting work — whether it's a limestone retaining wall, a neat paved driveway, or custom garden beds that suit WA's climate. Every job is quoted honestly and built to last.
02 / Service
Retaining Walls

Perth's sandy, sloped blocks — especially across Joondalup and the northern corridor — often need solid retaining walls to manage soil movement and make usable yard space. Rick's Trim & Brick builds limestone, brick and sleeper retaining walls that are engineered to handle the pressure and built to council spec. Whether you need a low garden retainer or a full multi-tiered wall on a steep block, the team delivers clean, durable results.

03 / Service
Paving

A well-laid paved area adds serious kerb appeal and functionality to any Perth home. Rick's Trim & Brick handles driveways, patios, pool surrounds and pathways using clay bricks, concrete pavers or natural stone — properly bedded and jointed so they stay put through WA's scorching summers. We work across Joondalup, Currambine, Burns Beach and surrounding suburbs, and every job includes a solid sub-base so your pavers won't sink or shift.

04 / Service
Brick Edging

Brick edging is one of the best investments you can make in a WA garden — it keeps lawn, mulch and soil where they belong, and it looks sharp year-round even in the dry Perth summers. Rick's Trim & Brick lays soldier-course and angled brick edging along garden beds, driveways and lawn borders using mortared bricks or dry-set techniques depending on the application. Clean lines, tight joints, built to last.

05 / Service
Garden Beds

From raised brick garden beds to wide limestone-bordered planting zones, Rick's Trim & Brick designs and builds garden beds that work with Perth's sandy soil and water restrictions. We shape, edge and prepare beds ready for planting — whether you're after a low-maintenance native garden, a vegetable patch or a lush tropical look. Joondalup gardens benefit hugely from properly structured beds that retain moisture and define your outdoor space.
