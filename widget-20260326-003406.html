Rick's Lawn Care — Baldivis' trusted green thumb

Rick's Lawn Care has been keeping Baldivis gardens lush and healthy for years. From weekly mowing runs through the estate to full reticulation installs and turf laying, Rick and his crew know exactly what it takes to keep a Perth garden thriving through the hot summers and wet winters.

Based right here in Baldivis, Rick is the local name residents trust when their lawns need serious attention — no call centres, no subcontractors, just honest work at a fair price.

Rick's Lawn Care is a fully owner-operated landscaping service based in Baldivis, servicing the southern suburbs of Perth. Rick brings a hands-on, no-fuss approach to every job — whether it's a quick tidy-up before a house sale or a complete backyard transformation. With a deep understanding of WA's sandy soils and hot climate, Rick ensures every lawn and garden gets the right treatment to stay green all year round.

02 / Service

Lawn Mowing — neat edges, every visit

Baldivis lawns can grow fast in the warmer months, especially after a good watering cycle. Rick offers reliable fortnightly and weekly mowing services across the suburb, including edging, trimming, and blowing down hard surfaces so your place always looks sharp.

Whether you've got a compact courtyard or a sprawling quarter-acre block with couch, buffalo or kikuyu grass, Rick tailors each visit to suit your lawn type and growth rate. Recurring clients get priority scheduling and consistent pricing all year round.

03 / Service

Reticulation — set and forget watering

In Baldivis, Perth's two-day-a-week watering roster makes a properly installed and timed reticulation system essential — without it, your lawn and garden beds will struggle through summer. Rick installs, services, and repairs retic systems across the suburb, including soaker hose conversions for garden beds and full pop-up systems for lawns.

Rick also handles fault-finding on existing systems — whether it's a broken head, a zone not firing, or a controller playing up. With Water Corporation guidelines front of mind, he'll make sure your system is compliant and efficient so you're not wasting a drop.

04 / Service

Garden Cleanup — back to its best

After a long Perth summer or a wet winter, garden beds can get out of hand fast — overgrown shrubs, weeds taking hold in the sandy Baldivis soil, and leaf litter piling up. Rick's garden cleanup service tackles it all: pruning, weeding, mulching, green waste removal, and a full tidy of all garden beds.

Popular for end-of-lease cleans, pre-sale spruces, and seasonal resets, Rick's cleanup jobs leave properties looking like new. Green waste is taken away and disposed of responsibly, so you're not left with a pile by the kerb.

05 / Service

Turf Laying — instant green results

New builds in Baldivis often come with compacted, sandy fill that needs proper preparation before turf can take hold. Rick handles the full process — soil prep, levelling, laying fresh turf, and first-water setup — so your new lawn has the best possible start in WA conditions.

Rick works with popular Perth turf varieties including Sir Walter Buffalo, Kikuyu, Couch, and Zoysia, helping you choose the right variety for your sun exposure and water budget. He coordinates with turf suppliers to get the freshest rolls on the day of install for the best establishment results.
