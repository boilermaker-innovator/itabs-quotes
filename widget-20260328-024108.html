02 / Service
Blocked Drains

Perth's old tree-lined suburbs can wreak havoc on your sewer lines — tree roots are the number-one cause of blocked drains in areas like Subiaco, Mount Lawley and Bayswater. Smith Plumbing uses high-pressure jet blasting and CCTV drain cameras to clear blockages fast and find the real cause. Whether it's a slow kitchen sink or a backed-up toilet, we'll have it sorted same day.

03 / Service
Hot Water Systems

A cold shower in a Perth winter is nobody's idea of fun. Smith Plumbing supplies and installs electric, gas and solar hot water systems across the metro area — and we service all major brands including Rheem, Rinnai and Dux. Whether your old storage tank has sprung a leak or you're upgrading to a modern continuous-flow gas unit, we'll have hot water back on within hours.

04 / Service
Gas Leaks

A gas leak is a serious emergency — if you can smell gas in your Perth home, turn off the meter and call Smith Plumbing immediately. As licensed gas fitters, we respond quickly to gas leak investigations, pressure tests and repairs on both natural gas and LPG systems. We also handle gas appliance connections for barbecues, stoves, heaters and outdoor kitchens — fully compliant with WA gas regulations.

05 / Service
Leaking Taps

A dripping tap in Perth wastes thousands of litres of water a year — especially important given our ongoing water restrictions. Smith Plumbing repairs and replaces all types of taps and mixers, from classic quarter-turn basin sets to modern concealed shower valves. We carry washers, O-rings and ceramic cartridges for all major brands, so most leaking tap jobs are fixed in a single visit.
