Colorbond
Fencing

Colorbond is the go-to fence choice in Rockingham — it handles the coastal salt air brilliantly and won't rot, rust or need painting like timber can. Pete's team supply and install genuine BlueScope Colorbond in the full range of colours, including popular Surfmist and Ironstone, at competitive per-metre rates. Whether you're replacing a tired old fence or securing a new build in Port Kennedy or Waikiki, we'll have it up fast and straight.

Timber
Fencing

Timber fencing adds a warm, natural look that Colorbond just can't match — perfect for rear gardens, front streetscapes and character homes across Rockingham. Pete uses quality treated pine and hardwood pickets that are treated to handle WA's harsh UV and summer heat. All our timber fences are set on proper concrete footings so they stay upright even in the sandy coastal soils common around the bay.

04 / Service
Pool
Fencing

Pool fencing in WA is governed by strict Building Commission regulations and Pete's Fencing Co knows every requirement inside out. We install glass frameless and semi-frameless pool fencing, as well as aluminium pool fencing, all fully compliant with AS1926. With so many Rockingham homes having backyard pools, getting a certified compliant pool barrier is essential — Pete's team takes care of all the paperwork and inspections too.
