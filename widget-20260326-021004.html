02 / Service

Colorbond Fencing

Colorbond is the go-to choice for Perth's southern suburbs — it handles the coastal wind, salt air and scorching summer sun without rotting, warping or needing a repaint. We supply and install the full BlueScope Colorbond range, including popular colours like Woodland Grey, Surfmist and Monument. Typical residential installs run from $85–$120 per metre, depending on height, terrain and any demolition of your existing fence.

03 / Service

Timber Fencing

A well-built timber fence adds real character to any Cockburn or southern suburbs home — great for front yards where street appeal matters. We use treated hardwood and pine palings that stand up to Perth's dry summers, and we can build lapped, capped or framed styles to suit your property. Timber fencing typically runs $70–$100 per metre, and we always treat the base of posts to handle our sandy local soils.

04 / Service

Pool Fencing

Pool fencing in WA must comply with the Building Act 2011 and Australian Standard AS 1926 — we know these regulations inside out. Whether you want frameless glass, semi-frameless or tubular aluminium, we'll design and install a pool fence that passes certification first time. Our pool fencing starts from around $120–$180 per metre, and we can organise the Building Permit if required.

05 / Service

Gates & Access

A quality gate makes your whole fence look the part. We build and hang Colorbond, timber and tubular steel gates in single or double configurations, and can also install automated driveway gates for added security and convenience. Pedestrian gates start from around $450 and double driveway gates from $750, supply and install — all using heavy-duty hardware that won't sag in Perth's summer heat.
