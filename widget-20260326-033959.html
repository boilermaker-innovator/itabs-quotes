Perth Decking Pros — Extend your living outdoors in Wanneroo

Perth Decking Pros are Wanneroo's trusted specialists in outdoor timber structures — from beautiful hardwood decks to sturdy colorbond carports. We bring professional craftsmanship and honest advice to every job, whether you're in Wanneroo, Two Rocks, or anywhere across Perth's northern suburbs.

With treated pine and premium hardwood options available, plus a free design consultation on every project, we'll help you create the outdoor space you've always wanted — built to handle Perth's harsh UV and coastal conditions.

02 / Service
Timber Decking

Nothing beats the warmth and character of a genuine timber deck for your Wanneroo home. We work with treated pine for budget-friendly durability and premium hardwoods like Merbau and Jarrah — both ideal for Perth's intense summer sun and occasional coastal winds.

Our timber decks are designed to last, with proper bearer and joist framing, quality decking boards, and stainless steel fixings that won't rust in the Perth climate. All builds comply with the relevant Australian Standards and Shire of Wanneroo requirements.

03 / Service
Composite Decking

Composite decking is the low-maintenance choice for Perth homeowners who want the timber look without the annual oiling and sanding. Modern composite boards are UV-stabilised, splinter-free, and engineered to handle temperature swings that push 45°C in Wanneroo summers.

We supply and install leading composite brands, all with long manufacturer warranties. Composite is also an excellent choice for pool decks and alfresco areas where wet feet and chlorine exposure would quickly degrade natural timber.

04 / Service
Deck Repairs

Perth's harsh UV rays and summer heat take a toll on older decks — rotting boards, failing bearers, loose fixings, and splintering timber are all common issues in Wanneroo and the northern suburbs. Don't let a deteriorating deck become a safety hazard for your family.

Perth Decking Pros offer thorough deck inspections and targeted repairs, from replacing individual boards to rebuilding entire subframe sections. We'll give you an honest assessment and only recommend the work that's actually needed — no unnecessary upselling.

05 / Service
Pergolas

A well-built pergola transforms your Wanneroo backyard into a genuine outdoor room — perfect for Perth's 8 months of outdoor-living weather. We design and construct freestanding and attached pergolas in treated pine, hardwood, or powder-coated steel to suit your home's style and budget.

All our pergola builds are engineered to meet the Shire of Wanneroo's building permit requirements, including wind load ratings for Perth's coastal conditions. We handle the paperwork and design so you can focus on planning your first backyard barbie under your new structure.

06 / Service
Carports

Protect your vehicle from Perth's scorching sun, occasional hailstorms, and overnight dew with a solid timber or steel carport. Perth Decking Pros build single and double carports that complement your home's architecture and meet all local council requirements in Wanneroo.

We offer a range of roofing options including Colorbond, polycarbonate and shade cloth to suit your budget and preferred level of light. Free design consultations mean you'll see exactly what you're getting before a single post goes in the ground.
