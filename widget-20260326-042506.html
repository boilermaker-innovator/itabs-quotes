01 / Perth Decking Pros

Perth Decking Pros — Extend your living outdoors

Perth Decking Pros are Wanneroo's trusted specialists in outdoor timber structures. Whether you're after a beautiful hardwood deck, a shaded pergola, or a solid carport, we bring craftsmanship and quality materials to every job. We use only treated pine and premium hardwood — built to handle Perth's fierce summers and year-round outdoor lifestyle.

02 / Service

Timber Decking

A quality timber deck transforms any Wanneroo backyard into a genuine outdoor living space. We work with treated pine and premium hardwoods like Merbau and Jarrah — species that stand up to Perth's hot, dry summers without warping or cracking. Our builds are engineered to council compliance with seamless integration into your existing home design.

03 / Service

Composite Decking

Composite decking gives you the look of natural timber with virtually zero maintenance — perfect for busy Wanneroo families. Our composite boards are UV-stabilised for Perth's intense sunlight, won't splinter, and are resistant to termites and moisture. Available in a range of woodgrain finishes to suit your home's style.

04 / Service

Deck Repairs

Perth's harsh UV and summer heat can take a toll on older decks — boards split, posts rot, and fixings corrode. Perth Decking Pros assess and repair existing structures across Wanneroo and the northern suburbs, replacing damaged boards, re-securing joists, and restoring your deck to a safe, great-looking condition. Don't replace what can be repaired.

05 / Service

Pergolas

A well-designed pergola creates shaded outdoor entertaining year-round — essential for Wanneroo's summer climate. We build timber pergolas in treated pine and hardwood, freestanding or attached to your home, with options for slatted roofing, shade sails, or polycarbonate panels. Council-compliant designs and a free consultation included on every pergola build.

06 / Service

Carports

Protect your vehicle from Perth's scorching sun and the occasional Wanneroo storm with a solid timber or steel carport. We design and build single and double carports to complement your home's architecture, using treated framing and durable roofing materials. All carports meet local council setback and height requirements.
