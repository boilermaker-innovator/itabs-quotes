Rick's Lawn Care — Baldivis' most reliable lawn & garden crew

Rick's Lawn Care has been keeping Baldivis gardens looking sharp since day one. From weekly lawn mowing on those fast-growing Perth summer lawns to full reticulation installs and turf laying — Rick and his crew handle it all, on time and at a fair price.

Rick's Lawn Care is a locally owned and operated landscaping business based right here in Baldivis, Southern Perth. Rick personally oversees every job — whether it's a quick mow and edge for a busy family or a complete garden transformation with new turf and reticulation. With fair pricing, no lock-in contracts, and a genuine love for making Baldivis lawns the best on the street, Rick's has built a reputation you can count on.
02 / Service
Lawn Mowing

Baldivis summers are relentless — buffalo and couch grass can shoot up a foot in a week when the Fremantle Doctor takes a day off. Rick's team mows, edges, and blows down your paths and driveway so your lawn looks tidy every single visit. Weekly, fortnightly, or one-off — we work around your schedule.

03 / Service
Reticulation

Perth's water restrictions make a well-designed retic system essential — and Baldivis soils can be unforgiving on lawns that miss even one watering cycle in January. Rick's team designs and installs reticulation systems that comply with the Water Corporation's two-days-per-week schedule, saving you money and keeping your grass alive all summer long.

04 / Service
Garden Cleanup

End-of-season blowouts, pre-sale tidy-ups, or just a garden that's gotten away from you — Rick's crew tackles overgrown verges, weedy garden beds, pruning, mulching, and rubbish removal. Baldivis properties can get very weedy very fast after a wet winter, and we'll have your place looking respectable again in a single visit.

05 / Service
Turf Laying

Whether you're building a new home in Baldivis or replacing a lawn that didn't survive the summer, Rick's lays fresh turf including Sir Walter Buffalo, Kikuyu, and Couch — varieties that thrive in Perth's sandy coastal soil and fierce UV. We prep the ground, lay and roll, and advise on the best watering schedule to get your turf established fast.
