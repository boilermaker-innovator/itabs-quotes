02 / Service

Blocked Drains

Joondalup's sandy soils and mature street trees are notorious for root intrusion and sediment build-up in stormwater and sewer lines. Smith Plumbing uses high-pressure jet blasting and CCTV drain cameras to find the blockage fast and clear it for good — not just a temporary fix. Most blocked drain jobs in the northern suburbs are resolved same day.

03 / Service

Hot Water Systems

Perth's hard water is tough on storage hot water units — scaling and corrosion shorten their life significantly. Whether you need an emergency replacement, an upgrade to an energy-efficient heat pump, or a solar hot water system to slash your bills, Smith Plumbing supplies and installs all major brands across Joondalup and the northern suburbs. We're also licensed to handle gas and electric units.

04 / Service

Gas Leaks

A gas leak is a plumbing emergency — don't wait. Smith Plumbing's licensed gas fitters respond urgently across Joondalup, Edgewater, Currambine and surrounding areas. We carry gas detection equipment on every vehicle, and all work meets WA's strict gas fitting regulations (AS/NZS 5601). If you suspect a leak, call us immediately on 0412 555 333.

05 / Service

Leaking Taps

A single dripping tap wastes thousands of litres of water per year — and with Perth's Water Corporation restrictions, that adds up on your bill fast. Smith Plumbing handles washer replacements, ceramic disc tap repairs, mixer tap overhauls and full tap replacements across Joondalup. We carry a wide range of quality fittings so we can complete most tap repairs in a single visit.
