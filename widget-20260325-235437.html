02 / SERVICE

Power Points & Switches

Upgrading your power points or adding new GPOs is one of the most common electrical jobs across Perth homes — and for good reason. Older Osborne Park homes often have dated cabling and insufficient outlets for modern appliances and home offices.

Sparky's can add USB-A/USB-C combo points, install double GPOs to replace singles, and fit smart switches throughout your home — all to current AS/NZS 3000 standards. No mess, no fuss, no guesswork.

03 / SERVICE

Switchboard Upgrades

Western Power and Energy Safety WA now require modern safety switches (RCDs) on all circuits in Perth homes. If your switchboard still has old ceramic fuses or a cramped single-phase board, it's not just outdated — it may not pass a home inspection.

Sparky's handles full switchboard replacements and upgrades across Osborne Park and surrounding suburbs, fitting circuit breakers, RCDs, and surge protection to bring your home up to current WA standards and handle today's electrical loads.

04 / SERVICE

Air Con Installations

Perth summers are brutal — Osborne Park regularly hits 40°C+ and a reliable split system isn't a luxury, it's a necessity. Sparky's handles the full electrical side of split system and ducted air conditioning installs, including dedicated circuits, isolator switches, and weatherproof cabling.

Working alongside your preferred installer or as part of a full package, Brad ensures your aircon is wired safely, efficiently, and ready for WA's punishing summer heat without tripping your board.

05 / SERVICE

Safety Inspections

Electrical faults are a leading cause of house fires in WA. Whether you're buying a new property in the northern suburbs, renting out an investment home, or just want peace of mind, a professional safety inspection from a licensed Perth electrician is essential.

Sparky's provides thorough electrical inspections covering switchboards, wiring condition, safety switch testing, smoke alarm compliance, and any visible hazards — with a written report you can use for insurance, real estate, or your own records.
