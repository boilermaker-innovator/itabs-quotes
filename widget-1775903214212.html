Why It Matters
Lawns Grow Fast in Perth
Perth's warm climate and long daylight hours mean grass can add several centimetres in a single week during spring and summer. Miss a couple of mows and you're suddenly dealing with a jungle that clogs mowers and hides weeds. Regular maintenance is the only reliable solution.
Context
The Hidden Cost of Letting It Go
An overgrown lawn doesn't just look bad — it attracts pests, makes weeding ten times harder, and can even violate council regulations in some suburbs. A garden cleanup on a badly neglected yard costs significantly more than regular maintenance. Staying on top of it is always the cheaper option.
Context
Perth's Lawn Challenge
The southern suburbs of Perth get intense UV, hot dry summers, and bursts of growth after rain. Couch and kikuyu grass — the most common types here — spread aggressively and need consistent trimming to stay manageable. Rick knows exactly how Perth lawns behave and schedules accordingly.
