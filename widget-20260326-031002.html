01 / Perth Decking Pros

Perth Decking Pros — Extend your living outdoors.

Wanneroo's trusted specialists in quality outdoor timber structures.

Perth Decking Pros are Wanneroo's go-to team for beautifully crafted outdoor decks, pergolas and carports. Whether you're after classic treated pine, durable hardwood or low-maintenance composite, we build outdoor spaces that add genuine value to your home and lifestyle.

We offer a free design consultation and work across all of Perth's northern suburbs — from Wanneroo to Joondalup and beyond. Every project is built to last in the Perth climate.

02 / Service

Timber Decking

Treated pine & hardwood options — built for Perth's harsh summer sun.

Perth's long, hot summers demand decking that won't warp, crack or fade. We use premium treated pine and Australian hardwoods — jarrah, merbau, and spotted gum — that are proven to handle the WA climate year after year.

From small alfresco additions in Wanneroo to full entertainer decks across the northern corridor, our timber decks are designed for durability, natural beauty, and real resale value. All work complies with WA Building Code requirements.

03 / Service

Composite Decking

Low-maintenance decking that stands up to WA's extreme sun and heat.

If you want the look of timber without the ongoing sanding, oiling and maintenance, composite decking is the smart choice. It won't splinter, fade or rot — perfect for Perth's intense UV exposure and the sandy soils of Wanneroo.

We install leading composite board systems with hidden fastener systems for a clean, seamless finish. Composite decks are also great around pools where barefoot safety and water resistance matter.

04 / Service

Deck Repairs

Restore your ageing deck before it becomes a safety hazard.

Perth's summer heat and winter storms take a toll on older decks. Rotting boards, loose posts and rusted fixings are common across Wanneroo's older homes — and they're a real safety risk for families and guests.

Perth Decking Pros carry out thorough deck assessments, replacing damaged boards, re-securing structures and restoring your deck to full structural integrity. We also refinish and reseal to give it a fresh, protected look that lasts.

05 / Service

Pergolas

Custom-designed shade structures that transform your backyard.

A well-designed pergola turns a bare patio into a proper outdoor room — perfect for Wanneroo's long alfresco season. We build freestanding and attached pergolas in treated pine and hardwood, designed to complement your home's existing architecture.

From open-beam designs to fully lined flat roofs, we handle the full build including council permits where required. Free design consultation included so you can plan your ideal outdoor space before you commit.

06 / Service

Carports

Protect your vehicle from the WA sun with a quality timber carport.

Perth's UV index is among the highest in Australia — and leaving your vehicle exposed takes a real toll. A quality timber carport adds shade, protection and real street appeal to your Wanneroo property.

We build single and double carports using treated pine and hardwood frames, designed to meet WA Building Code standards. Many of our Wanneroo carport builds also include integrated pergola areas for a functional, great-looking result.
