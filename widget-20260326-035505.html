Extend your living outdoors — Wanneroo's deck specialists

Perth Decking Pros have been transforming Wanneroo backyards into stunning outdoor spaces for years. Whether you're after a classic hardwood deck, a low-maintenance composite solution, or a beautiful pergola to beat the WA sun, we bring the craftsmanship and materials to make it happen. 02 / SERVICE

Timber Decking

Nothing beats the warmth and character of natural timber underfoot. We work with premium treated pine, jarrah, and Merbau hardwood — all chosen for their durability in Perth's harsh UV and heat. Wanneroo homes love a timber deck that wraps around the alfresco, and our team handles everything from footings to the final coat of Cutek oil.

03 / SERVICE

Composite Decking

Composite decking is the smart choice for Perth homeowners who want a beautiful outdoor space without the ongoing maintenance. Our composite boards resist fading, splintering, and the intense summer heat that beats down across the northern suburbs. Ideal around pools, on rooftop terraces, and in coastal-adjacent areas like Wanneroo where salt air can age natural timber faster.

04 / SERVICE

Deck Repairs

Perth's UV-intense summers and occasional wet winters can take a real toll on older decks — rotting boards, wobbly posts, and faded surfaces are all common in Wanneroo homes built in the 90s and early 2000s. We assess, quote, and repair quickly so your outdoor space is safe and looking great again. Board replacements, re-stumping, sanding and re-oiling — we handle it all.

05 / SERVICE

Pergolas

A well-built pergola turns a plain backyard into a year-round outdoor living zone — essential in Perth where you're entertaining outdoors ten months of the year. We design and construct freestanding or attached pergolas using treated pine or hardwood, sized and positioned to make the most of your block. Council approval assistance available for Wanneroo City builds.

06 / SERVICE

Carports

Protect your vehicle from Perth's punishing summer sun with a quality timber or Colorbond carport. We build single and double carports to match your home's style, ensuring all work meets WA building codes and City of Wanneroo requirements. From a simple single-bay cover to a full double carport with integrated storage, we'll design and price it up at no cost.
