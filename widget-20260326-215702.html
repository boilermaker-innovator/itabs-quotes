About Perth Fencing Co

Perth Fencing Co has been serving Osborne Park and the greater Perth metro area for over 15 years. We started as a one-man operation and have grown into one of the most trusted fencing companies in the region — built on word of mouth and honest work.

We work on residential and commercial projects of all sizes, from a simple side-gate replacement to full boundary fencing on new builds. Our crews know Perth's soil conditions, council setback requirements, and the right products to handle WA's scorching summers.

Every job comes with a free measure and quote, and all our work is backed by a comprehensive workmanship guarantee. We don't cut corners, we don't disappear after the invoice — we stand behind every post we sink.

Colorbond Fencing

Colorbond is the go-to fencing choice across Osborne Park and Perth for good reason — it handles the heat, the wind, and the salt air without warping, rotting, or rusting. We install full Colorbond fence lines including cappings, posts, and infill panels to Australian standards.

We carry the full range of Colorbond colours so your new fence can complement your home's existing palette. Whether you're after the classic Ironstone or something lighter like Surfmist, we'll help you choose what works.

All our Colorbond installations come with a workmanship guarantee, and the steel itself carries BlueScope's product warranty. It genuinely is a set-and-forget fencing solution for Perth conditions.

Timber Fencing

Nothing adds character to a Perth backyard quite like a well-built timber fence. We use treated pine and hardwood options depending on your budget and aesthetic — all treated to handle WA's UV intensity and seasonal moisture swings.

Our timber fences are built to last, not just look good on day one. We pay attention to proper post depth, correct concrete mix, and weatherproof capping to ensure the structure performs for years without sagging or leaning.

Timber is particularly popular in Osborne Park's established residential streets where homeowners want a natural look that blends with mature gardens. We can also treat or paint to your preferred finish as part of the job.

Pool Fencing

Pool fencing in WA is governed by strict Building Act regulations and Australian Standard AS 1926. We know these requirements inside out and install pool fencing that passes council inspection first time, every time — no costly re-visits.

We install frameless glass, semi-frameless glass, aluminium slat, and Colorbond pool fence options depending on your preference and budget. All hardware, latches, and gate mechanisms are compliant and tested before we leave the site.

If you're in Osborne Park or nearby suburbs and have recently had a pool installed or are buying a property with an older pool fence, we offer a compliance inspection and quote service. Don't risk a fine or worse — get it right from the start.

Gate Installation

A fence is only as good as its gate, and we install swing gates, sliding gates, and pedestrian access gates in Colorbond, aluminium, and timber to match any fence style. Every gate is hung level, operates smoothly, and is built to take daily use.

We also supply and install gate hardware including heavy-duty hinges, self-closing mechanisms, keyed locks, and child-safe latches — all important if you have a pool, young kids, or a dog that knows how to push a gate open.

For properties across Osborne Park and Perth's northern suburbs, we can custom-fabricate gates to fit non-standard openings. If you're replacing a tired old gate or adding one to an existing fence line, we can quote the job on its own.
